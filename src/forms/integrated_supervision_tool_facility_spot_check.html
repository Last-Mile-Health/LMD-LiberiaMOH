Integrated CHSD / DEOH / NHPD Supervision Spot Check Tool - v0.30 Health Facility
This tool is to be used by enumerators to conduct spot checks for integrated supervision visits. Spot checks should occur quarterly, within two weeks of the original supervision visit, in counties that have conducted integrated supervision visits. Enumerators conducting spot checks should find the CHSS originally interviewed during the integrated supervision visit and fill out the following tool. Inform the CHSS you are conducting a follow-up assessment to verify the accuracy of the data collected during the first visit, and make sure the CHSS is aware that he/she was selected randomly for this verification and not as a result of his or her original answers.
